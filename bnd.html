I love But Sometimes because it captures emotions I never quite knew how to express out loud. The line "Mascara smeared from crying every day" speaks so vividly to the quiet, messy heartbreak—the kind you try to hide but shows anyway. It’s more than just sadness; it’s exhaustion from feeling too much. "When I hit the ground, I feel so empty inside" echoes the hollowness I’ve felt after being let down over and over, like all the pain led to nothing but silence. And when they sing "Oh, I could’ve died for you", it strikes something deep in me. That kind of love, selfless, painful, almost foolish, is so real. The line "Oh, does it seem like a waste? Why are you selfish?" feels like finally confronting someone who took everything from you without realizing what they meant to you. This song doesn’t just sound beautiful. It hurts beautifully. And sometimes that’s exactly what I need to hear.
