I love Cyberpunk because it feels like stepping into another world, dark, electric, and emotionally charged. The moment the song starts, it wraps you in this neon-lit atmosphere, like you're walking through a futuristic city filled with secrets and pain. The heavy synths and layered vocals create this sense of isolation and longing, yet there’s strength in it too, like pushing forward even when everything feels mechanical and broken. The lyrics speak to a kind of emotional numbness and rebellion, something that hits deep when you're trying to survive in a world that feels cold and unfeeling. It’s not just a song. It’s an experience, one that makes you feel seen when you’re stuck in your own quiet battles. ATEEZ didn’t just make music with Cyberpunk, they built an entire feeling.
