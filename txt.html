I love I Know I Love You because it feels like a cry from the depths of loneliness, wrapped in raw emotion and teenage desperation. The moment Seori’s haunting voice opens the song, it instantly pulls me into this cold, chaotic world that TXT paints, a place where everything feels meaningless except for one person. The lyrics are simple but powerful, like “In this world of zero, I know you're my one and only”, and that line hits especially hard when you’ve ever felt like you were drowning but someone became your reason to breathe. The rock-influenced sound adds this wild, messy energy that mirrors the chaos inside, making it feel so real and relatable. It’s a love song, yes, but it’s also a song about survival, about clinging to something pure in a world that feels broken. And that’s why I keep coming back to it. It understands the kind of love that saves you.
